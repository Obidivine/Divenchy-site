WNBA star addresses online rumors, chooses to keep relationship private

The Indiana Fever guard says her focus remains on basketball, not public speculation about her personal life.

By Staff Writer | FTW USA Today  •  November 18, 2025

Caitlin Clark has become one of the most recognizable figures in women’s basketball, drawing national attention for her play on the court and her growing influence off it. With that visibility, however, has come increased speculation about her personal life — something the WNBA star says she prefers to keep private.

In a recent statement, Clark explained that she has intentionally chosen not to share details about her relationship, citing the impact that constant online attention can have on those closest to her.

“I love the support and passion from fans,” Clark said. “But my personal life is something I want to protect. I don’t want the internet interfering with a relationship that’s important to me.”

Clark’s comments come amid widespread online rumors linking her to various people — claims she says are inaccurate and distracting.

“There’s a lot of rumors out there about me dating different people,” Clark said. “I want to clear the air by saying that I am officially engaged to my husband, who is a firefighter and a chief. That part of my life is private, and I’m happy keeping it that way.”

While Clark acknowledged the curiosity that comes with public success, she emphasized that maintaining boundaries allows her to stay focused on her career and personal well-being.

As her professional journey continues, Clark says she hopes fans will respect those boundaries and keep the spotlight where she believes it belongs — on the game.
